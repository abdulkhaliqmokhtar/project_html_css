Nasi Lemak Recipe.

Delicious Malaysian nasi lemak, served with sambal, fried anchovies, fried peanut, sliced cucumber and hard-boiled egg.

Ingredients

  • 3 cups rice
  • coconut milk, estimate the quantity yourself (in Malaysia, we call 'agak-agak')
  • 3 shallots
  • 1 teaspoon fenugreek
  • 1 stalk lemongrass
  • 1 inch garlic, thin sliced
  • 1 thumb ginger, mashed
  • 1 pandan leave, shred and node
  • A little bit of oil for saut√©
  • Adequate salt

Directions

  1. Wash the rice and drain.
  2. Heat the oil on the stove and fry the onion, ginger, fenugreek, lemongrass and pandan leave until the onion is soft and fragrant.
  3. Add the rice and stir for a while.
  4. Add coconut milk until the water is one finger's phalange length.
  5. Add salt to taste and stir a bit so that the salt dissolves evenly.
  6. Close the lid and transfer to an electric rice cooker. If not, you can continue cooking on the stove until cooked.
