Nasi Ayam.

My favorite food of all time! Don't be fooled by the simple ingredients. The secret is in the broth: this is the most amazing, flavorsome chicken rice dish you've ever tried. Garnish with sliced cucumber, spring onions, and cilantro.

Ingredients

For the Chicken:

  • 1 whole chicken
  • 1 stalk spring onion (optional)
  • 3 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 1 thumb fresh ginger root (optional)
  • Water to cover
  • Salt to taste

For the Soy Sauce:

  • 5 tablespoons light soy sauce
  • 1 and a 1/2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 1 tablespoon garlic oil (optional)
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil

For the Rice:

  • 3 cups uncooked rice
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced, or to taste
  • 1 thumb ginger, crushed
  • Salt to taste

For the Garlic Chile Dip:

  • 5 red chile pepper, seeded and chopped
  • 2 limes, juiced
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • 1 thumb ginger (optional)
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 pinch white sugar, or to taste

Ginger Dip:

  • 2 limes, juiced
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • 1 thumb young ginger (optional)
  • 1/8 teaspoon salt

Directions

  1. Trim chicken of all excess fat, reserving fat.
  2. Place chicken, spring onion, 3 cloves crushed garlic, and one thumb ginger in a large pot. Pour in enough water to cover. Bring to a boil; reduce heat to low and simmer, skimming any scum off the surface, until an instant-read thermometer inserted into the thickest part of the thigh, near the bone, reads 74 degrees Celsius, about 1 hour.
  3. Remove chicken from the pot, reserving broth. Rub chicken with salt. Chop into pieces and arrange on a serving plate.
  4. Melt reserved chicken fat in a large pot over medium heat. Add rice, 3 cloves minced garlic, crushed 1 thumb ginger, and salt; cook and stir until rice is glossy and fragrant, 2 to 3 minutes.
  5. Pour enough reserved chicken broth into the pot to cover rice by half an inch. Cover; bring to a boil. Cook until rice is tender and liquid is absorbed, 20 to 25 minutes. Let rice sit, covered, for 5 minutes. Fluff with a fork.
  6. Combine red chile peppers, juice of 2 limes, 2 tablespoons reserved chicken broth, 3 cloves garlic, one 1/2-inch piece ginger, 1/4 teaspoon salt, and 1 pinch sugar in a blender; blend until garlic chile dip is smooth.
  7. Combine juice of 2 limes, 2 tablespoons reserved chicken broth, 3 cloves garlic, one thumb piece ginger, and 1/8 teaspoon salt in a blender; blend until ginger dip is smooth.
  8. Serve garlic chile and ginger dips alongside chicken and rice.
